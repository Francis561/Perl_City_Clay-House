Quilled Wreath Pop-Up

Friday, November 5
5pm-7pm

Paper quilling is suddenly everywhere, and we’ve got a perfect introductory project to get you started. You can check out some sample wreaths at the beautiful boutique/gallery downtown!

For $35 you can join Deb at ArtCloth + Craft to create one of a kind quilled hoop wreaths or ornaments. We will cover the basics, and supply all you need to create a small 3 or 4 inch wreath, but we will have extra kits and supplies on hand so you can take what you’ve learned and apply it to a larger project.

Holiday Table Runner Pop-Up

Thursday, November 11
5pm-7pm
at Tammarie Designs

We are so excited to be teaming up with another local creative to bring you this opportunity to create a one of a kind heirloom table runner to add to your holiday table.

We will be joining Tammy of Tammarie Designs in her studio for a special make and take event.

Tammy will share her sewing expertise to help students create a holiday themed table runner. Once sewn Deb will help students add a holiday-themed block print to the runner to complete it. The finished table runner can be further personalized by adding family signatures around the block during your holiday celebrations. Class cost is $40.

Autumn Landscape

with guest artist Leslie Calimeri

Join us online for these VIRTUAL Fall Classes

Book Bag WorkShop

Friday, November 19
6:30pm-9:30pm
Virtual Zoom Event

Join us via zoom to take some things you probably already have at home, some printer paper, thread and a gift bag, and turn them into this cute little book complete with handles! We will use a few simple tools to create this book, bound with this long stitch style binding, complete with a button. Its a great way to use that gift bag from a vacation that you’ve been saving, and the finished book is perfect to give as a gift or keepsake, or keep it for yourself, the handles make it convenient to carry anywhere!

You don’t need any previous experience, and we will send you a full list of materials when you sign up. The zoom recording will be available after the workshop with lifetime access.
